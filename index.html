KICODEP

About US

Kisauni Community Development Programme is Non-Government Organizations (NGO) roistered by The Non-Governmental Organizations Co-ordination Board to run its operation in Kenya. The organization started to operate since 11-Mar-10. The Non-Governmental Organizations Co-ordination Act (Cap 19) of 1990 established the Non-Governmental Organizations Co-ordination Board as a State Corporation.

Empowering communities

Mission

KICODEP mission is to:

  • Advocate for social justice

    We raise awareness of issues affecting marginalized communities and advocate for positive change.

  • Strengthen communities

    We promote community engagement and collaboration to address local challenges.

  • Promote sustainable development

    We implement initiatives that are environmentally friendly and economically viable.

Vission

Instead of a static vision statement, KCDP proudly presents a vibrant vision board, a living representation of our aspirations for the Kisauni community. This board is updated regularly to reflect the evolving needs and aspirations of the community we serve. It features images, quotes, and symbols that represent a future where:
